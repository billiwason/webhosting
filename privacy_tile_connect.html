저장된 정보는 다음 목적으로만 사용됩니다:

본 앱은 사용자의 정보를 제3자와 절대 공유하지 않습니다.

Privacy Policy

Tile Matching app does not collect any personal information from users.

1. Information We Store

The app only stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game Progress: Challenge mode stage completion data
  • Sound Settings: Sound effects On/Off preference
Important: This information is stored only on your device (SharedPreferences) and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. Use of Information

The stored information is used only for:

  • Saving and loading game progress
  • Maintaining user preferences

3. Third-Party Sharing

The app never shares any user information with third parties.

4. Internet Connection

The app does not require an internet connection and works completely offline.

  • No network permissions requested
  • No data transmission
  • No server communication

5. Advertising and Analytics

The app:

  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not use analytics tools
  • Has no tracking features

6. Permissions Required

The app does not require any special permissions.

  • No camera permission

  • No storage permission

While the app does not collect sensitive information, stored data is protected by Android's standard security mechanisms.
